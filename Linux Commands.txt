*** Lesson 21: Wildcards

(* - 0 or more characters, ? - 1 single character, [] a range of characters
\ - as an escape character, ^ the beginning of the line, $ - the end of the line)
touch abc{1..9}-xyz :tạo file từ abc1-xyz, abc2-xyz, ..., abc9-xyz
